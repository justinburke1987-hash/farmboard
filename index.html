The Problem

The whiteboard works —
until it doesn't.

  • Someone adds a task when you're in the field — you don't see it until dinner
  • Handwriting gets wiped, tasks get lost, nobody remembers who was supposed to do what
  • Busy season hits and coordination breaks down exactly when it matters most
  • No record of what got done — or what you were doing this same week last year
  • Every farm hand needs to be at the board to know the plan for the day
How It Works

Three steps.
That's the whole thing.

01
📱
TEXT THE NUMBER

Any trusted farm hand texts your farm's dedicated number. No app, no login, no training. If they can text, they can add a task.

02
📄
IT SHOWS UP

The task appears on the board instantly — on the shop TV, your phone, a tablet in the cab. Everyone on the farm sees it in real time.

03
✓
CHECK IT OFF

Tap to complete. Done tasks are archived automatically, building a searchable history that surfaces what you were doing this same week in prior years.

Features

Built for the shop, not a startup.

High-contrast, readable from across the room. Designed for shop TVs, phone screens, and tablet mounts on equipment.

📱
SMS & Telegram In

Tasks come in via text or Telegram. Only trusted numbers on your allowlist can add tasks — no spam, no strangers.

📺
Shop TV Mode

One-tap fullscreen. Designed to be readable at 15 feet. Put it on the shop TV and the whole crew stays in sync without touching it.

📋
Seasonal History

The History tab shows what you were doing this exact week in prior years. Built-in institutional memory for pre-season prep.

👤
Named Farm Hands

Every task shows who added it and when. Phone numbers map to real names so Roger's tasks show as Roger, not a 10-digit number.

🔴
Priority Flagging

Mark any task urgent. Priority tasks sort to the top with a red indicator so critical jobs never get buried under routine work.

⚡
Real-Time Updates

No refreshing. When a task comes in or gets checked off, every screen on the farm updates instantly via live data subscriptions.

📅
Key Differentiator
"What were we doing this week last year?"

Every farm has institutional knowledge that lives in someone's head. FarmBoard captures it automatically. When pre-planting week hits, you'll see exactly what you checked off in the same window last year — and the year before. No other tool does this for farms.
